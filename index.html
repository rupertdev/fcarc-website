Welcome to the Franklin County Amateur Radio Club

Welcome to the Franklin County Amateur Radio Club. If you are interested in joining the club and would like to attend a meeting, the club meets on the first Monday of each month at the Forks Cafeteria located at 339 Brooks St, Wake Forest, NC 27587 - Come eat at 6pm with the members and the meeting starts at 7pm and generally runs through to around 8pm.

Weekly Club Nets on 2m and HF

Franklin County Amateur Radio Club operates a weekly net Wednesday nights at 8pm on the 146.715 repeater.

Net participants are welcomed to give a ragchew in addition to the week's discussion topic. All licensed radio amateurs are invited to check in.

Daryl Cash K4DCP holds a weekly HF net in the General Voice portion of 80m.

Join Daryl and other local hams Tuesday night at 7:30 pm EST on or around 3.850 MHz +/- 5 kHz dependent on QRM.
